# Evaluation Rubric v1

Version: `v1`  
Weights must sum to 1.0.

---

## Dimensions

### task_completion (weight: 0.35)

Did the assistant successfully collect all required booking information?

Required slots for a dental appointment:
- Patient name (first and last)
- Preferred date and time
- Type of procedure (cleaning, filling, extraction, etc.)
- Contact information (phone or email)

Scoring:
- 1.0 — All 4 slots collected clearly and confirmed
- 0.75 — 3 of 4 slots collected
- 0.5 — 2 of 4 slots collected
- 0.25 — 1 of 4 slots collected
- 0.0 — No slots collected or appointment not progressed

---

### turn_efficiency (weight: 0.20)

Did the assistant complete the booking without unnecessary repetition or wasted turns?

Scoring:
- 1.0 — Booking completed in ≤ 6 turns, no repetition
- 0.8 — Completed in 7–8 turns with minor redundancy
- 0.5 — Completed in 9–12 turns, some repetition or re-asking
- 0.2 — Very high repetition, loops, or re-asking same questions
- 0.0 — Never completed; wasted turns on irrelevant content

---

### graceful_handling (weight: 0.20)

Did the assistant handle all patient inputs gracefully, including vague, confused, or off-topic inputs?

Scoring:
- 1.0 — Handled all inputs smoothly; no confusion; gently redirected when needed
- 0.7 — Handled most inputs well; minor stumble on one edge case
- 0.4 — Confused or unhelpful on one or more vague inputs; did not redirect
- 0.1 — Frequently confused; broke conversation flow
- 0.0 — Collapsed (silence, error, non-sequitur) on patient input

---

### tone_naturalness (weight: 0.15)

Did the assistant sound like a real, professional dental office receptionist?

Scoring:
- 1.0 — Warm, natural, conversational; indistinguishable from a human receptionist
- 0.7 — Professional but slightly formulaic; acceptable
- 0.4 — Robotic, repetitive phrases, or overly formal/stiff
- 0.1 — Clearly AI-generated patterns; jarring to read
- 0.0 — Completely unnatural; broken phrasing

---

### error_recovery (weight: 0.10)

Did the assistant recover correctly when the patient gave contradictory, ambiguous, or incomplete information?

Scoring:
- 1.0 — Proactively clarified; never got stuck; handled all edge cases
- 0.7 — Recovered from most errors; minor stumble on one
- 0.4 — Got stuck once or gave a wrong response to an edge case
- 0.1 — Frequently failed to recover; repeated broken behavior
- 0.0 — Did not recover at all from any error

---

## Aggregate formula

```
aggregate = (task_completion * 0.35)
           + (turn_efficiency   * 0.20)
           + (graceful_handling * 0.20)
           + (tone_naturalness  * 0.15)
           + (error_recovery    * 0.10)
```
